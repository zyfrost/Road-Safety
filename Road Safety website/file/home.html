Introduction

Road safety is important for everyone. By following a few simple rules, we can all do our part to prevent accidents and keep our roads safe.

Pedestrians

As a pedestrian, you should:

  • Use sidewalks and crosswalks whenever possible
  • Look both ways before crossing the street
  • Avoid walking distracted (e.g. using your phone)
  • Wear reflective clothing or accessories at night to increase visibility

Cyclists

As a cyclist, you should:

  • Wear a helmet at all times
  • Ride in the same direction as traffic
  • Use hand signals when turning
  • Avoid wearing headphones or using your phone while cycling

Motorists

As a motorist, you should:

  • Always wear your seatbelt
  • Follow posted speed limits
  • Avoid distracted driving (e.g. using your phone)
  • Be mindful of cyclists and pedestrians on the road
